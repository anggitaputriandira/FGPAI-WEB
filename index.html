Read About FGPAI

The Islamic Religious Education Teacher Forum is an association of PAI teachers who discuss material to share on social media or with their students. Various materials from PAI teachers must be negotiated before being uploaded to the website, so that there is no misunderstanding in terms of the material. The material on this website is provided by PAI teachers who have professional education or who have taught Islamic teachings. The material on the Islamic Religious Education Teacher Forum website aims to increase Muslim understanding and faith, thereby making Muslims know several Aqidah or Fiqh sciences.
